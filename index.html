Privacy Policy

App Name: Limitless Calculator

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Introduction

Limitless Calculator is a calculator app designed to perform big-number calculations. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

Information We Collect

Limitless Calculator does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal user data.

How the App Works

All calculations are performed locally on the user's device. The app does not send calculation data to any server.

Calculation History

If calculation history is available in the app, it is stored only on the user's device. This history is not uploaded, shared, or sent to any external server.

Internet Access

Limitless Calculator does not require an internet connection for its calculator features.

Third-Party Services

The current version of Limitless Calculator does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Limitless Calculator does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
