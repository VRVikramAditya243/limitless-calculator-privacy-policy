Privacy Policy

App Name: Limitless Calculator

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Introduction

Limitless Calculator is a calculator app designed to perform big-number calculations. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information and what third-party services may process when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Limitless Calculator does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or email address through the app.

Calculator inputs, results, and history are processed and stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated in this policy.

How the App Works

All calculator operations are performed locally on the user's device. The app does not send calculation values or history to a personal server operated by the developer.

Calculation History

If calculation history is available in the app, it is stored only on the user's device. This history is not uploaded, shared, or sent to the developer's own servers.

Internet Access

Limitless Calculator's core calculator features do not require an internet connection. However, the app may use internet access for advertising services provided by Google AdMob.

Advertising

Limitless Calculator may display banner advertisements using Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect, process, and share certain device-related data in order to provide ads, measure ad performance, improve ad services, and help prevent fraud or abuse.

This may include information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interaction information, diagnostics, and approximate information related to ad delivery.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. Through AdMob, Google may collect and process certain data in accordance with its own privacy policies and terms.

For more information about how Google uses information from apps that use its services, please review:

Data Stored Locally

The app may store calculator history and app preferences locally on your device so that features such as history, display settings, or formatting preferences continue to work properly.

Data Sharing

Limitless Calculator does not sell your personal data. However, third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob may process and share certain limited device-related data as part of ad delivery, reporting, fraud prevention, and related advertising operations.

Children's Privacy

Limitless Calculator is not directed specifically to children and does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Your Choices

Users may be able to manage ad personalization and advertising preferences through their device settings or Google settings, where available.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly.
